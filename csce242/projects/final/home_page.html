Birds of USC

Explore Our Feathered Friend Community

Birds are everywhere.

Thriving in forests, deserts, grassland, tundra, Antarctic, suburbia-- even right here at USC. This website is intended to serve as a resource for exploring the beautiful bird community we have on campus, hosted by our local Audubon chapter: Midlands Bird Society. Explore the huge diversity of birds that inhabit campus through a (soon to be) comprehensive list of local species, real on-campus photography, and links to additional resources on birds altogether.

Midlands Bird Society is a local campus chapter of National Audubon Society, a national organization that advocates for birds. See more on the "About MBS" page.
